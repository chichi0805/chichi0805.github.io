About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Intelligent Power Electronics and Electric Machine Lab (IPEM Lab) at the University of Georgia. My research focuses on cyber-physical security, anomaly detection, contrastive learning, motor drive systems, and power electronics control. I work on bridging real-world hardware testbeds with physics-guided machine learning algorithms.

Research Interests
